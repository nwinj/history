*Who were the early nationalists.

A group of leaders from the educated middle-class who believed and open-minded and moderate politics. They asked for constitutional and other reforms within the framework of British rule as they had faith in the British sense of justice and fair play

*What were the beliefs of the early nationalists.

1.they had faith in the sense of justice, Fairplay, honesty and integrity of the British.

2. They hoped to be granted home rule.

3.they believed that the British rule aided in cleansing social ills like sati, untouchability, and Child marriage.

4.They thought that the British would help Indian govern them selves according to Western standards.

5. The main obstacle to India's progress was social and economic backwardness and not the British colonial rule.

6.they relied on constitutional and peaceful methods to achieve their aims
